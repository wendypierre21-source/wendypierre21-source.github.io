Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Hype Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Hyperion Uploader application ("the Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 TikTok Authentication Data

  • OAuth 2.0 Access Tokens and Refresh Tokens: Issued by TikTok when you authorize the Application. Stored locally on your device.
  • TikTok User ID and Display Name: Basic profile information (user.info.basic scope) to confirm your identity.

1.2 Video Content and Metadata

  • Video Files: Read from your local device for uploading to TikTok. Not stored or copied beyond the upload process.
  • Video Metadata: Title, description, hashtags, privacy settings, and publishing parameters you provide.

1.3 Application Usage Data

  • Upload History: A local log of videos uploaded, including timestamps and TikTok video IDs.

2. How We Use Your Information

DataPurpose
OAuth TokensAuthenticate with TikTok's API and upload video content on your behalf
TikTok User ID / Display NameVerify your identity and display account information
Video FilesTransmit content to TikTok for publication via the Content Posting API
Video MetadataConfigure and complete the publishing process
Upload HistoryProvide you with a record of publishing activity

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate external servers or cloud storage for your personal data.

  • OAuth tokens stored in local configuration files accessible only to the Application.
  • No data transmitted to any server other than TikTok's official API endpoints.
  • All API communication over HTTPS (TLS-encrypted).

4. Third-Party Services

  • TikTok (ByteDance Ltd.): Data transmitted directly to TikTok's servers during upload. Governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate with analytics services, advertising networks, or other third-party data processors.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information. Data may only be disclosed:

  • To TikTok: As necessary to upload videos via TikTok's API.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or court order.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: All data is stored locally and directly accessible to you.
  • Deletion: Delete all data by removing the Application's configuration files.
  • Revocation: Revoke access through TikTok account settings.
  • Portability: Data stored in standard file formats, transferable at your discretion.

EEA residents may have additional rights under GDPR. California residents may have additional rights under CCPA. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Data Retention

Data retention is entirely under your control since all data is stored locally. We do not maintain remote copies.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The Application is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA).

9. International Data Transfers

Video data is transmitted to TikTok's servers, which may be located worldwide. By using the Application, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes are indicated by the "Last updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
