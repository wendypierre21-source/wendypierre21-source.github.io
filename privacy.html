Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Hyperion Uploader is a local desktop application. We do not collect or store personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

2. TikTok OAuth

We use TikTok's OAuth 2.0 to authenticate users. We access your TikTok account only to publish videos you explicitly authorize. We store your OAuth tokens locally on your device.

3. Data We Access

  • user.info.basic: Your TikTok username and display name, to confirm identity.
  • video.upload / video.publish: To upload and publish videos on your behalf.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. No analytics or tracking is used.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via the GitHub repository.
